Lesson 1: Differentiating Edible and Poisonous Mushrooms
On the left we have true morel mushrooms and on the right we have false morel mushrooms.
  • True morel mushrooms are edible.
  • Uniformly shaped cap
  • Pitted intwards
  • Covered in pits and ridges
  • Attached directly to stem
  • False morel mushrooms are not edible.
  • Irregularly shaped
  • Bulging outwards
  • Wavy and lobed
  • Hangs freely from the stem
  • When sliced in half, a false morel mushroom will not be hollow, it is filled with cotton-like white fibers inside.
